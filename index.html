Campaign Role Intelligence — Introducer / Assist / Closer 90D Fixed
Business Question
Which campaigns introduce, assist, and close conversions — and what does each campaign's role mean for budget decisions?

Role determined by Assist Ratio (assists ÷ last-click conversions). Pure Introducer: zero last-click, top-of-funnel only. Introducer (>1.5): more assists than closes. Assist (0.5–1.5): balanced mid-funnel. Closer (<0.5): predominantly bottom-of-funnel.

Methodology
◆ How This Attribution Analysis Works
Data Source
All data comes directly from Google Ads via the Google Ads Scripts API. Campaign stats are refreshed every 30 minutes via GitHub Actions. Attribution data (assist ratios, MTA ROAS) is pulled from your account's path-of-conversion reports.
Attribution Model
Last-click revenue reflects what Google Ads reports in the Campaigns view. Multi-touch attribution (MTA) adds assisted revenue — value from campaigns that appeared in the conversion path but didn't receive the last click.
Conversion Tiers
Tier 1 = direct revenue (Offline Invoice, Website Purchase). Tier 2 = appointment pipeline (London In-Store, Manchester, Virtual, Contact Form). Tier 3 = engagement signals (WhatsApp, Checkout begins) — intent indicators without assigned revenue value.
